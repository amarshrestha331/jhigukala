Our Story

The Beginning of Jhigu_Kala

It was the second phase of lockdown. Three teenagers, stuck at home, wanted to do something meaningful. One of us suggested starting a small business from home, and another noticed the growing trend of scrunchies online. Inspired, we met the next day, planned everything, and launched our brand Jhigu_Kala on December 19, 2020.

We began by selling to family and friends. The response was good at first but slowly declined. That’s when we came up with a new idea, helmet scarfs we personally needed and believed other rider girls would too.

Since then, we’ve been selling helmet scarfs across and beyond the Kathmandu Valley, turning a lockdown idea into a lasting venture.
